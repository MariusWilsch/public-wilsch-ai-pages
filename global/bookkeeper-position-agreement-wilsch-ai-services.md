## Result Statement

**Define the result this position is accountable for producing:**

> To ensure correct and compliant financial data flows through Xolo — invoices, expenses, receipts, and payments — through oversight of AI Bookkeeping Agents, compliance verification, and exception handling, so that operational finance runs without founder intervention.

**"Correct" defined:** Passes compliance verification checklist, all Xolo inputs complete and accurate, payment discrepancies resolved.

---

## Work Listing

**List all strategic and tactical work for which this position is accountable.**

### Strategic Work (ON the position)

1. Design invoice creation workflows (inputs, format, compliance checks)
2. Design AI agent delegation rules (what is automated vs human-reviewed)
3. Design and improve expense tracking and receipt management systems
4. Create and maintain compliance checklists for the Operations Manual
5. Evaluate automation approaches and iterate on failed attempts
6. Provide input to VP / Finance on financial operations and friction points

### Tactical Work (IN the position)

1. Review and approve AI agent-generated invoices before client delivery
2. Handle true exceptions agents cannot resolve (human judgment calls, one-off entity decisions, legal disputes)
3. Final compliance verification on financial outputs
4. Escalate unresolvable issues to VP / Finance

**Delegated to AI Bookkeeping Agents:**
- Invoice creation (gather inputs, fill Xolo fields, attach documents)
- Xolo platform communication (email-based interactions, translations)
- Payment discrepancy detection and resolution
- Expense receipt forwarding and categorization
- Routine platform administration

---

## Standards

**List quantity, quality, and behavior standards for which this position is accountable.**

### Position-Specific Standards

1. Every invoice must pass the compliance verification checklist before client delivery — no exceptions.

2. All financial inputs to Xolo must be complete and accurate before submission. Incomplete data creates downstream compliance risk.

3. AI agent outputs must be reviewed by the Bookkeeper before any client-facing delivery. The human is the final quality gate.

4. Payment discrepancies must be flagged and resolved promptly. Unresolved mismatches are escalated to VP / Finance.

5. Compliance checklists in the Operations Manual must be kept current as new rules are discovered (e.g., Leistungszeitraum requirement added after first German B2B invoice).

6. Exception handling decisions must be documented for future reference — one-off judgments become reusable knowledge for AI agent training.
