## 5. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the EU.

Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved in the courts of the EU.

---

## 6. Limitation of Liability

### 6.1 Liability Cap
Service Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total amount paid by Client under this Agreement.

### 6.2 Consequential Damages
Neither party shall be liable for consequential, indirect, incidental, special, or punitive damages arising from this Agreement.

### 6.3 Force Majeure
Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform obligations due to circumstances beyond their reasonable control (force majeure events).

---

## 7. Warranties and Representations

### 7.1 Service Provider Warranties
The Service Provider warrants that:
- It has the expertise and capability to perform the Services
- Services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner
- It will comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- It has no conflicts of interest in performing the Services

### 7.2 Client Warranties
The Client warrants that:
- It has the right to provide access to systems and documentation as required for Services
- It has full authority to enter into this Agreement
- It will provide accurate information and timely feedback as needed for Services

---

## 8. General Provisions

### 8.1 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements, discussions, and understandings relating to the subject matter hereof.

### 8.2 Amendments
Any modifications to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

### 8.3 Assignment
Neither party may assign this Agreement or any rights or obligations hereunder without the prior written consent of the other party.

### 8.4 Severability
If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

## 10. Intellectual Property

### 10.1 Client Ownership
All work product, deliverables, and materials created specifically for Client under this Agreement become the exclusive property of Client upon full payment.

### 10.2 Service Provider Pre-Existing IP
Service Provider retains all rights to:
- Pre-existing intellectual property, tools, frameworks, and methodologies
- General knowledge, skills, and experience gained during the engagement
- Non-client-specific code libraries and components developed independently

### 10.3 License Grant
Client receives a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use any Service Provider pre-existing IP incorporated into the deliverables solely for the purposes for which the deliverables were created.

Service Provider retains the right to use general concepts, methodologies, and non-confidential knowledge gained from this engagement in future work for other clients.

- Deliver all completed work products
