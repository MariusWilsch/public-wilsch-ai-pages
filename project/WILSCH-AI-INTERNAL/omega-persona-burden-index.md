# Omega Persona — "Secretly Wants to Be a Singer"
[[client-omega]]

Target avatar for the AI Video Clipping Service. Psychographic profile with burden index scoring each problem by severity and service relevance.

---

## Dream Outcome

"I finally become the singer I've always secretly wanted to be. I can sing confidently, move people, and friends/family see me as a genuinely good singer — not just someone 'trying'."

---

## Burden Index

Each problem is scored on two dimensions:

- **Severity:** How heavily this burdens the avatar (🔴 High / 🟡 Medium / 🟢 Low)
- **Service Relevance:** Does the clipping service address this? (✅ Direct / 🔗 Indirect / ⬜ None)

**Direct** = the clipping service produces content that directly addresses this problem.
**Indirect** = clips normalize or model the behavior that resolves this problem.
**None** = outside the service scope (programme/coaching addresses this, not clips).
