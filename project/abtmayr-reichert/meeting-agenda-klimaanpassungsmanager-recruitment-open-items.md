## Discussion Topics

*Starting points for discussion, not limited to these.*

### 1. Project Plan — Milestones, Responsibilities, September Target
⏱️ 20 min

Ralph proposed creating a project plan with goals, responsibilities, and milestones — targeting September 2026 as the major checkpoint. The design doc now covers five pipeline parts across two sides (student recruitment + municipal acquisition). Each part needs an owner and a timeline.

- Five pipeline parts need responsibility assignments: Agenda, Stellenbeschreibung, LinkedIn, Platform, Municipal Acquisition
- Michael emphasized parallel execution — student and municipality acquisition running simultaneously
- Ralph's operational role (Geschäftsführer) suggests he coordinates across parts while Michael handles external relationships (PIK/UBA, municipalities)
- Marius/team handles technical execution and content production

**To resolve:** A milestone plan with owners and target dates for each pipeline part, working backward from September 2026.

### 2. DAS Funding Eligibility for Werkstudent and Übungsleiter Positions
⏱️ 15 min

The DAS-Förderrichtlinie funds "Fachpersonal" in "befristete Stelle für Klimaanpassungsmanagement." The program is designed for full-time professionals at TVöD EG 10–11. Michael confirmed this is his responsibility to research — "Das ist eine zentrale Aufgabe, die muss ich noch lösen."

- The design doc now includes three employment paths: Werkstudent (20h), Übungsleiter (10–12h, 400–500€), Pflichtpraktikum
- The Förderrichtlinie does not explicitly prohibit non-traditional arrangements
- Michael's Berlin contact (PIK/UBA) may have insight into whether DAS covers any of these paths
- If DAS doesn't cover these positions, municipalities fund from own budget — changing the value proposition significantly

**To resolve:** The funding path for each employment type — whether DAS, municipal budget, or a hybrid model — and what Michael's contacts advise.

### 3. LinkedIn Content Approval Workflow
⏱️ 5 min

Marius/team will produce LinkedIn content for the Klimafolgenschutz e.V. company page. Michael gave carte blanche on content creation — but the approval workflow was never explicitly resolved. With Ralph now involved as Geschäftsführer, the approval chain may include him.

- Content pillars are defined (Klima-Wissen, Karriere, Gemeinden, Verein)
- Posting cadence is 1–2x per week
- Some posts reference Michael's PIK contacts and municipal partnerships — these may need review
- Ralph has been added as additional LinkedIn admin (access level TBD)

**To resolve:** Whether posts go through Michael, Ralph, both, or neither before publishing — and whether this varies by content pillar.

### 4. Municipal Product Packaging — Top 3 Adaptation Measures
⏱️ 15 min

The design doc now includes Part 5 (Municipal Acquisition) which requires concrete "products" for municipalities — packaged adaptation measures with scope, cost, and funding information. Michael is waiting on his Berlin contact for the three most frequently funded measures.

- Candidate measures: Gewässerschutz, Hitzeschutz, Rückhaltebecken, Ausgleichsflächen, Bepflanzung/Innenstadtgestaltung
- Each product needs: measure description, typical Gemeindegröße, required disciplines, estimated effort, applicable Förderquote
- Michael's analogy: product tiles on the website, similar to the E-Bike Ladestation

**To resolve:** The first three product packages to build — based on Michael's Berlin data or team prioritization if the data hasn't arrived.
