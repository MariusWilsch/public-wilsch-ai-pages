## SLA.2 Service Levels

### SLA.2.1 Coverage Hours

- **Business Hours:** 08:00–18:00 CET, Monday to Friday, excluding German public holidays
- **Availability:** Service Provider guarantees coverage only during these hours
- **Outside Business Hours:** Service Provider is unavailable and has no obligation to respond until the next business day

### SLA.2.2 Priority Levels

**Business-Critical:**
- Definition: Issues preventing essential operations or transactions
- Examples: System cannot process transactions, data loss occurring, complete service outage

**Non-Critical:**
- Definition: Partial disruptions that do not fully block operations
- Examples: Sluggish performance, minor interface glitches, cosmetic issues

### SLA.2.3 Response Times

- **Business-Critical issues** reported within coverage hours: Response within **2 hours**
- **Non-Critical issues** or incidents reported outside coverage hours: Best-effort basis (typically next business day)

### SLA.2.4 Resolution Time

**Best Effort:** No guaranteed deadline is provided. Actual times depend on complexity, third-party dependencies, and resource availability.
