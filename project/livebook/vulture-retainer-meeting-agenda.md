## Discussion Topics

*Starting points for discussion, not limited to these.*

### 1. Vulture's scope has evolved faster than its specification
⏱️ 15 min

Vulture moved from V1 exclusion (Mar 10) to priority one (Mar 14) in four days. The technical documents describe a growth automation engine. The conversations describe content production. Both are valid visions — they are not the same build.

- Mar 10: Vulture excluded from V1, no pushback
- Mar 14: "That's priority one" — complete reversal
- Specs: self-evolving funnels, A/B testing, cultural signal detection
- Conversation: "producing me content, marketing content"
- March Technical Plan build order places Vulture at position 5 of 6

**To resolve:** What Vulture V1 actually produces — content format, platforms, niches — so a concrete build scope can be defined.

### 2. The orchestrator retainer — extraction, design, developer system
⏱️ 15 min

Marius positions as the orchestrator of EasyBooks' AI development — not as a solo developer building against undefined requirements. The retainer covers extraction (turning Stafford's vision into buildable specs), developer orchestration (a system that makes any developer 2-3x productive), and continuous improvement.

- Marius's capacity: multiple concurrent clients, cannot commit full-time development
- The contradictions in the current specs mean any developer coding now would build the wrong thing
- Three-Slice Ownership: Marius designs + improves, developers build, Stafford's hire operates
- Week 1 is a design sprint: 2-3 hours with Stafford to define Vulture V1 concretely
- Developers (David + Stafford's people) run through the same system

**To resolve:** Whether this engagement model works for Stafford — and the retainer terms that make it viable for both sides.

## Meeting Format

- **Type:** Proposal presentation + alignment
- **Expectation:** Marius presents the contradictions and orchestrator model. Stafford decides yes/no on the retainer.
- **Outcome:** Start signal — retainer begins with week 1 design sprint, or engagement ends cleanly.
