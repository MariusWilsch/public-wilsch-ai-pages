## Source 5: EB Business Master Plan (Stafford's Document)

**Google Drive ID:** `1l7AhojY2FjvOZh1IMnugbKuLLNBpnbH4owv991hraSQ`
**Title:** "EB - My 2026 Business Master - Easybooks"

### Section: Social Media Funnel System

> "We build multiple parallel micro-funnels."

> "Funnel Type 1: Emotional Trigger Funnels
> These are short-form hooks on:
> Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, YouTube Shorts
> Examples:
> 'Tell us about the moment that changed your life.'
> 'What would your 18-year-old self think of you today?'
> 'If this was your last chapter, what would it say?'
> CTA: 'Answer this in 2 minutes.'
> They click → land on a 2–3 question AI micro-interview.
> Output: Instant mini-chapter preview.
> Psychological effect: They've already started their book."

> "Funnel Type 2: Founder Journey Funnel
> Target: entrepreneurs
> Ad example: 'Your company has a story. Have you captured it yet?'
> They enter: Why you started, The hardest moment, The turning point
> AI outputs: A 1-page Founder Origin Story PDF.
> Hook: 'Want to continue your journey into a full book?'
> This turns a founder into: Recurring narrative client."

> "Funnel Type 3: Corporate Insight Funnel
> For B2B:
> Offer: 'Turn 3 customer interviews into a branded case story.'
> Entry: Upload call transcript.
> Output: Structured narrative case study.
> Then upsell: Internal corporate story vault."

> "Funnel Type 4: Legacy Funnel
> Target: Parents, grandparents.
> Ad: 'If your children had one book from you, what would it say?'
> Low-friction voice entry.
> This funnel has extremely high emotional conversion."

### Section: Automated AI Funnel Expansion Engine

> "A. AI Funnel Builder
> AI constantly:
> Analyses which prompts convert best
> Detects trending emotional themes
> Builds new funnel variations automatically
> A/B tests messaging
> Rewrites ad copy
> Funnels become self-evolving."

> "B. Behaviour-Based Funnel Extension
> If someone completes: 1 mini interview,
> AI automatically:
> Suggests related chapters
> Suggests sequel topics
> Sends email nudges
> Creates 'unfinished story' reminder
> It expands the narrative surface area."

> "C. Social Content Repurposing Engine
> From Interesting Conversations:
> Clips are auto-created
> Quotes are extracted
> Emotional moments are highlighted
> These become:
> Shareable snippets
> Quote graphics
> Teaser audio clips
> Every completed book becomes a marketing funnel itself."

### Section: AI Lifecycle Growth Engine

> "The system constantly measures:
> Funnel click rate
> Interview completion rate
> Drop-off points
> Depth score
> Preview generation
> Purchase intent
> AI then:
> Rewrites prompts
> Adjusts emotional tone
> Shortens friction points
> Optimises CTA placement
> Funnels become dynamic."

### Section: Social Loop Flywheel

> "1. User creates book.
> 2. Shares preview snippet.
> 3. Friends click emotional funnel.
> 4. They start mini interview.
> 5. AI delivers preview.
> 6. They convert.
> Books become ads. Stories become funnels."

### Section: High-Level Funnel Architecture Diagram

> → Micro Interview (Opera)

## Source 7: EasyBooks Design Doc v0.3 (Marius/DaveX2001)

**Published:** https://mariuswilsch.github.io/public-wilsch-ai-pages/project/livebook/easybooks-design-doc

### Section 2.1 — Vulture

> "Vulture discovers audience opportunities and generates campaign creatives. It is the traffic engine — without Vulture, users must be acquired manually. Vulture makes acquisition systematic and self-improving.
>
> Responsibilities:
> Detect cultural niche opportunities (trending topics, emotional moments, seasonal triggers)
> Generate campaign creatives (hooks, scripts, video concepts) per niche
> Distribute across platforms (Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, YouTube Shorts)
> Measure performance and iterate daily — funnels become self-evolving
>
> Core schemas:
> niches: id, name, category, score, trend_signals, created_at
> creatives: id, niche_id, type (hook/script/video), content, variant_label, performance_score, created_at
> campaigns: id, creative_id, platform, status, budget, impressions, clicks, conversions, created_at
>
> North Star Metric: Click → Interview Start Rate (Vulture creative → Plug micro-interview)
>
> Governance:
> Human approval gate for paid campaigns — no automated spend without review
> Cultural sensitivity flags + escalation queue
> Brand guardrails (no manipulative content, no sensitive-targeting abuse)
> Rate-limit posting automation to avoid platform violations"
