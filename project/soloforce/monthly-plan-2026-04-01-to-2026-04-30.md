### ARCHIBUS -- €3,720/month | Retainer through Aug 2026

Stable anchor. This project inspired the Design Partner model -- it's the proof case for what the business should look like. SWG (University of Worcester) is an expansion vector where the client has the same problem: selling software isn't timely anymore. Already in communication with two commercial teams. Could increase monthly revenue AND unlock the success-based fee that's been undefined since November.

- **Contract:** Retainer (mix), stable through August
- **Design Partner fit:** Origin of the model. Closest to target state.
- **Open:** Success fee undefined. SWG expansion requires JA design beyond current scope.

### IITR -- €6,336 in March | T&M @€120/hr

Biggest surprise. Won the client after resolving a legal dispute. Nice hourly rate, new project already ordered for next month. Scope expanding into avatar/on-premise AI research beyond the original DS-Kit RAG.

- **Contract:** T&M @€120/hr. New project ordered for next month.
- **Design Partner fit:** Low. Project-by-project relationship — they order, we deliver. Classic agency model.
- **Open:** New project contract needs drafting.

### UWI -- €3,050/month | Retainer €2,500 + SLA €550

More partner than client. Extended month-to-month because the design partner contract isn't ready yet. Underpriced for the work being done. Carries Malaysia expansion, Haseloff Partnership, AI-in-a-Box, hardware sales -- all through Ulrich. May decision point coming.

- **Contract:** Month-to-month extension. Stopgap.
- **Design Partner fit:** Already a partner in practice. Contract doesn't reflect it.
- **Open:** May renegotiation. Needs #847 design partner contract.

### ABTMAYR-REICHERT -- €2,610 in March | T&M @€120/hr

Solid work through Casa Curata UG. David implementing, design reviews flowing. Had one project this month, another ordered for next.

- **Contract:** T&M hourly. Wants retainer -- blocked on #847.
- **Design Partner fit:** Strong. Environmental/Klimafolgenschutz work is cross-departmental by nature -- fits the Design Partner model well compared to IITR's more narrow technical scope.
- **Open:** Retainer conversion.

### AVO -- €33,500 POC offer signed | Fixed-price

Infrastructure block resolved (DGX Spark ready). May booking target if pushed hard. Goes through Ulrich/WPH. R&D lab for autonomous AI coding on IBM Power.

- **Contract:** Fixed-price POC. 23 days @ €1,300/day + €3,600 hosting.
- **Design Partner fit:** Not applicable -- project-based.
- **Open:** Push to start. May revenue target.

### REKERS -- €2,300 WS2 deposit | Now a lead

Workshop 2 delivered and 40% deposit invoiced. POC conversion is the next step. Connected to AVO through shared IBM Power infrastructure and Ulrich/Thomas relationship.

- **Contract:** WS2 complete. POC next (lead).
- **Open:** POC proposal and conversion.

### ROHDEX -- €720 one-off | Paused

Migration invoiced. Client paused -- reactivation expected Q2/Q3 when they have bandwidth.

- **Contract:** None active. Hourly when needed.
- **Open:** Nothing until they re-engage.

### TRACELINE -- Done

Absorbed into Design Partner model. No longer a standalone project.
